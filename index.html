Florida is considered one of the premier destinations for all things diving as many people already know. What you may not know is the variety of dive sites that can be found in florida. These sites include: - Freshwater Springs - Sinkholes (new/old) - Manmade Reefs/Breakwaters - Offshore Wrecks - Offshore Reefs - and many more! This site will help you explore some of the most well known diving spots in florida and also give you access to locations that only the locals know.
